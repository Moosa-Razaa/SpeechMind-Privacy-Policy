Privacy Policy for SpeechMind

Last updated: 4th-June-2025

Introduction

SpeechMind ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: Our app requires microphone access to provide core speech processing functionality. This policy explains how we handle your audio data.

Audio Recording and Processing

What We Collect

  • Audio Recordings: Our app records audio through your device's microphone for speech processing and analysis purposes.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app's audio features.

How We Use Audio Data

  • To provide core app functionality (document creation and analysis)
  • To enhance user experience and app performance

App Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Required for core audio recording and speech processing functionality
  • Internet Access: For cloud-based speech processing and app functionality

Data Retention

We retain your audio data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Audio recordings are typically processed and then deleted within 24-48 hours unless you explicitly save them within the app.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
